# Unicode Glyphs
  Glyph | Code  | Use
 ------:|:------|-----
      ➔ | 2794  | Menus, sub-items, links.
      ← | 2190  | Left arrow.
      ↑ | 2191  | Up arrow.
      → | 2192  | Right arrow.
      ↓ | 2193  | Down arrow.
      🛆 | 1f6c6  | Warning.
      🛇 | 1f6c7 | Danger. Stop.
      🛈 | 1f6c8 | Informational.
      ⛑ | 26d1 | Rescue.
     🗘 | 1f5d8 | Waiting / Working / Processing.
      ✔ | 2714  | Success / Enabled / On.
      ✘ | 2718  | Failure / Disabled / Off.
      ⋮ | 22ee  | Additional context (or context menu).
      ⚙ | 2699  | Settings.
      ⌘ | 2318  | Super key.
    LMB | LMB   | Left click (Left mouse button).
    MMB | MMB   | Right click (Right mouse button).
    RMB | RMB   | Middle click (Middle mouse button).
      ✋ | 270B  | Hover the mouse over the indicated location next to this symbol.
    🔇 | 1F507 | Mute.
