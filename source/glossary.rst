.. _glossary:

Glossary
########

.. glossary::

  LBM
    Left mouse button. This means left click.

  RMB
    Right mouse button. This means right click.

  ✋
    Hover mouse. Hover the mouse over the indicated location next to this
    symbol.

    u+270B
    https://www.fileformat.info/info/unicode/char/270b/index.htm

  ⌘
    The **super** key: this is the **windows key** or **command key**. May also
    be referred to as ``win``, ``command``, ``super``.

    u+2318
    https://www.fileformat.info/info/unicode/char/2318/index.htm

  ☐
    Unchecked box. The correct setting for the specified option is unchecked or
    disabled. May be a box, radio, or binary slider (on/off) in the GUI.

    u+2610
    https://www.fileformat.info/info/unicode/char/2610/index.htm

  ☑
    Checked box. The correct setting for the specified option is checked or
    enabled. Can be a box, radio, or binary slider (on/off) in the GUI.

    u+2611
    https://www.fileformat.info/info/unicode/char/2611/index.htm

  ›
    GUI navigation action separator. Indicates the following items is the next
    element to look for while navigating the GUI.

    u+203A
    https://www.fileformat.info/info/unicode/char/203a/index.htm

  ⋮
    Vertical Ellipis. Generally used in mobile for an alternative drop-down menu
    with more options.

    u+22EE
    https://www.fileformat.info/info/unicode/char/22ee/index.htm

  ' '
    Punctuation Space. Blank space used to hold a position where no text is
    required, but a non-whitespace value must be present for content to be
    generated correctly. Typically used for config tables at then end of a data
    List. (See :ref:`core-switch-vlan-setup`).

    u+2008
    https://www.fileformat.info/info/unicode/char/2008/index.htm

  UBNT
    Ubiquiti Networks. This is a common Acronym used for their devices.

  Unifi
    A line of products from UBNT.
